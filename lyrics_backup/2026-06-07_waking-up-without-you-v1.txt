[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
(I reach across the pillow where you used to lay your head)
(Now there's nothing but the shadows lying with me in this bed)
(The morning light feels heavy when it's shining on the truth)
(I am burning in the quiet, waking up without you)

[Verse 1]
(The coffee's pouring cold, the fire's burning down to ash)
(I close my eyes and I can see the memory in a flash)
(The way you pulled my collar down, the secret in your smile)
(I want to keep you wrapped in my arms, and hold you for a while)

[Pre-Chorus]
(Every tick of the clock is a drum beating in my chest)
(Every breath that I take is a sigh that I can't suppress)
(I am craving the taste of your lips, the slip of your hand)
(You are the only desire that I will ever understand)

[Chorus]
(Waking up without you is a slow and aching burn)
(A fever in my blood, a fire at every single turn)
(I need the rhythm of your heart beating right against my own)
(With you in my arms is the only home I've ever known)

[Post-Chorus]
[Echo]
(Waking up without you, burning up without you)
(I am lost in the morning, waking up without you)

[Verse 2]

(A sweet and heady poison, letting all the wildness in)
(I remember the slide of your touch, the whisper in the dark)
(Leaving every piece of me shattered by a single spark)

[Bridge]
(I need the sweet salvation of the skin you are living in)
(I am begging for the start of where you end and I begin)
(You know the fire never settles when the craving takes the lead)
(It is an endless burning hunger, a promise from Saeed)
(I will pull you through the distance, I will conquer all the space)
